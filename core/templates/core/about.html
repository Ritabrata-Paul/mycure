Our Journey

How MyCure360360 evolved from an idea to the leading healthcare appointment platform

2020

The Beginning

MyCure360360 was founded with a vision to revolutionize healthcare appointment booking. The idea was born from our founder's personal experience with the inefficiencies of traditional healthcare scheduling.

2021

Platform Launch

After months of development and testing, we launched our platform with partnerships with 15 healthcare providers across 3 cities, offering seamless appointment booking services.

2022

Expansion & Growth

MyCure360360 expanded to 10 more cities and partnered with over 50 healthcare facilities. We also introduced new features like health records management and telemedicine options.

2023

Technology Advancement

We integrated AI-powered recommendations and enhanced our platform with mobile applications for both iOS and Android, making healthcare even more accessible.

2024

National Recognition

MyCure360 received national recognition as the leading healthcare appointment platform, serving over 50,000 patients across 25+ cities with more than 500 healthcare providers.

2025

Future Vision

We're now working on international expansion and enhancing our platform with predictive health analytics and personalized healthcare recommendations.
